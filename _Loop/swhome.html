September 27th

Jackhammering on A Level of Regenstein Tuesday and Wednesday

We are finding that the sound of the jackhammering on the A-level is being heard throughout Regenstein.

Accordingly, I wanted you to know that Vicki has put copies of the attached sign on the doors leading into the reading rooms and into the faculty study wing and also near the elevator buttons for the main passenger elevators. Copies have also been posted at the entry control point and at the Ask A Librarian Desk.

As indicated on the sign, earplugs are available from Circulation staff.

Please email Jim Vaughan if you have questions on this.

New Exhibition: Poetic Associations: The 19th-Century English Poetry Collection of Dr. Gerald N. Wachs

Romantic and Victorian poets were celebrities and, often, close friends, part of a literary community that influenced their professional and personal lives. Dr. Gerald N. Wachs collected their works, seeking whenever possible copies that were presented by the author to other writers, friends, or family members.
