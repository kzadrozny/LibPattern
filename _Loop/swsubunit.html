Ghoul Studies

Related Units: Alchemy | Metallurgy | Herbology | Defense of the Dark Arts

The Circulation section of the Dark Arts Division is responsible for book lending and general customer service. This includes checking in and out books, whether stacks or reserve material, and, as circulation workers often serve as the library's "human face," answering patron questions about their account, the library and library policies.
